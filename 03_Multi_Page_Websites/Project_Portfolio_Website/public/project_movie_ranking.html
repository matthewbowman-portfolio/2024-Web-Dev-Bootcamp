The Best Movies According to Matthew

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time:

The Matrix

This movie was pivitol and mind blowing when I first saw it as a child.
I fell in love with the concept of our reality being set in a computer simulation.

Ex Machina

Next-Level Sci-fi/Horror movie with great acting and thought-provoking story-telling.

Blade Runner 2049

Very beautiful and artistic film set in a semi-apocalyptic future with captivating technology.
